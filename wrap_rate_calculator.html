How to use this calculator

  1. Start with Direct Labor = 1.0000 to calculate your wrap rate multiplier.
  2. Adjust the rate percentages to match your actual indirect rates.
  3. FBLC (Fully Burdened Labor Cost) is your break-even floor — the minimum you must recover before any profit.
  4. Add your Fee on top of FBLC to get your Billing Rate to the Government (FBLF).
  5. Once you know your multiplier, replace 1.0000 with any hourly wage to see the fully-loaded billing rate for that employee.
